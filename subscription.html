Teacher Subscription

Welcome to our platform! To ensure that your profile is visible to students, we require a subscription. You can choose from different subscription plans:

Subscription Details:

Duration: 8 weeks

Cost: 50 AED

Duration: 1 year

Cost: 250 AED

Subscription benefits include:

  • Visibility on the platform for students to find and book lessons.
  • Access to update your profile and add lesson times and availability.
  • Increased opportunities to connect with students and grow your teaching profile.

How to Subscribe:

1. Choose a subscription plan (8 weeks or 1 year).

2. Make a payment for your selected plan, and we will activate your subscription.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us through the support section.
