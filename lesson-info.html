Lesson Booking Information

Each lesson costs 100 AED/hour.

  • Online Lessons: Must be paid online. Students and teachers will discuss payment in the chat.
  • In-Person Lessons: Students can pay by cash or card.
  • If paying by card in-person, teachers must provide their bank account to receive payments.
  • Teachers will receive 85 AED per lesson; 15 AED (15%) goes to the platform.
  • All communication, including lesson details and payment, will happen in the Chats page.
